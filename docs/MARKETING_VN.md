## 1. Situation Analysis

### Why Vietnam, Why Now

Vietnam is one of the fastest-growing IELTS markets in Southeast Asia. Demand is driven by:

- **Study abroad** — over 190,000 Vietnamese students studying overseas (2025 estimate), the majority needing IELTS 6.0–7.0
- **Migration** — Canada, Australia, UK visa routes require IELTS; demand accelerated post-COVID
- **Corporate requirement** — tech companies (FPT, VNG, Axon, global outsourcing firms) list IELTS scores in senior engineering JDs
- **Promotion criteria** — many mid-large tech companies require Band 6.5+ for principal/lead/manager roles interacting with overseas clients

Vietnam's software engineering workforce is estimated at 530,000+ (VINASA 2025) — the primary beachhead segment. Beyond engineers, hundreds of thousands of Vietnamese professionals in healthcare, finance, education, and business also need IELTS for immigration and career mobility, and face the same mismatch problem.

### The Gap in the Market

Existing IELTS tools (IELTS.org practice tests, IELTS Liz, British Council prep) are:
- Generic — built around everyday topics (food, travel, environment) irrelevant to working professionals
- Passive — no AI feedback loop
- Expensive for full prep courses (VND 5–15M per course)

This app fills a structural gap: **AI-driven IELTS prep that uses the learner's own domain as the learning context** — leveraging knowledge they already own instead of forcing them to start from zero with unfamiliar topics. Software engineers are the primary initial segment; the platform is designed to serve any professional preparing under time pressure.

## 2. Target Segments

### Primary: Mid-Level Software Engineers (2–7 YOE)
- Need IELTS for visa applications or job changes to international teams
- Already comfortable with English reading; weak on Speaking and Writing Task 2
- Motivated but time-poor — need efficient, context-relevant practice
- Device: primarily laptop/desktop during work; mobile for commute

### Secondary: Senior Engineers & Tech Leads (7+ YOE)
- Need IELTS for PR (Australia/Canada) or leadership roles requiring client communication
- Higher willingness to pay
- Influential in recommending tools to their teams

### Tertiary: CS/IT Students (Final Year)
- Preparing for grad-school applications or first jobs at global companies
- Price-sensitive; strong word-of-mouth potential within university groups

### Expansion: Other Professionals with Domain Depth
- **Healthcare workers** (nurses, pharmacists, doctors): need IELTS for overseas registration
- **Finance & banking professionals**: need IELTS for international roles or study abroad
- **Education sector**: teachers applying for overseas programmes or higher degrees
- Shared profile: existing professional expertise + specific IELTS deadline + time-poor
- Acquisition: LinkedIn, professional association groups, immigration advisor referrals

## 3. Positioning

**Tagline (EN):** "IELTS prep that meets you where your knowledge already is."
**Tagline (VI):** "Luyện IELTS bằng kiến thức bạn đã có — AI chấm điểm, không phải giáo viên."

**Core message:** Bạn đã có kiến thức chuyên môn sâu trong lĩnh vực của mình. App này biến thứ đó thành lợi thế — dùng chủ đề bạn nghĩ đến mỗi ngày (dù là hệ thống phần mềm, quy trình bệnh viện, hay báo cáo tài chính) để xây dựng vốn từ vựng, collocations, và cấu trúc bài IELTS mà examiner đánh giá cao. Bạn không cần học lại từ đầu — bạn cần đúng công cụ.

**Primary market message (engineers):** "Học bằng chủ đề tech bạn đã biết rõ — không mất thời gian học lại những thứ không liên quan."

## 4. Distribution & Channel Strategy

### 4.1 Facebook — Primary Acquisition Channel

Facebook remains the dominant social platform in Vietnam (~70M MAU). Key tactics:

**Facebook Groups (organic, high priority)**
- Target groups with 50k–500k members and high engagement:
  - IELTS Vietnam (1.2M members)
  - IELTS Fighter Community
  - Cộng đồng IELTS Việt Nam
  - Vietnam Software Engineer Community
  - ITviec Community
  - Lập trình không khó
- Share weekly value posts: sample essays graded by AI, vocabulary lists for tech topics, speaking answer breakdowns
- Do NOT post "sign up" links directly — build trust over 4–6 weeks first, then soft-pitch

**Facebook Page + Reels**
- Post short AI-graded essay examples (before/after format)
- Speaking demo clips: engineer reads a Part 2 answer, AI scores it in real time
- Aim for 3–4 posts/week; boost top performers with VND 200k–500k/post to test reach

### 4.2 YouTube — Authority Building

YouTube is the primary long-form learning platform in Vietnam after Facebook.

**Content series ideas:**
- "Từ vựng IELTS cho lập trình viên" (IELTS vocabulary for developers) — 5–8 min/episode
- "Chấm bài Writing Task 2 bằng AI" — screencast showing the app grading a real essay
- "Mình đạt 7.0 Speaking như thế nào sau 3 tháng" — testimonial format (recruit early users)
- "Luyện Speaking với AI — không cần giáo viên" — product demo

Target: 1 video/week for 3 months. SEO keywords: "luyện IELTS miễn phí", "IELTS writing AI", "IELTS speaking luyện tập".

### 4.3 Zalo — Retention & Community

Zalo is Vietnam's dominant messaging app (50M+ MAU) and the preferred channel for community groups over WhatsApp.

- Create a **Zalo Official Account** for broadcast messages (new features, tips, weekly challenges)
- Create a **Zalo Group** (up to 500 members) for early adopters — direct feedback loop
- Use Zalo Mini App capability (long-term) to embed a lightweight version of the practice feature

### 4.4 LinkedIn — B2B & Senior Segment

LinkedIn is growing among Vietnamese tech professionals, especially those targeting international roles.

- Share "IELTS for engineers" thought-leadership posts (English)
- Connect with tech leads, engineering managers, CTOs at outsourcing firms
- Reach out to HR/L&D teams at Axon, FPT Software, KMS Technology, Nashtech, Persistent, Bosch Vietnam — offer team licenses

### 4.5 TikTok — Awareness for Younger Segment

Short-form video with strong virality for the CS student segment.

- 60–90 second clips: "AI grades my IELTS essay in 10 seconds"
- "IELTS vocabulary bạn cần biết nếu làm IT"
- React/duet format with existing IELTS content creators

### 4.6 Dev Communities (Organic, High Trust)

- **Viblo.asia** — Vietnamese developer blog platform; publish technical + learning articles
- **ITviec Blog** — career-focused articles; pitch a guest post on "IELTS for tech careers"
- **TopDev** — developer job board with community content
- **Reddit r/learnvietnamese** and **r/IELTS** — English-language international audience (secondary)
- **Spiderum** — long-form Vietnamese content platform popular with young professionals

## 6. Feedback Collection Plan

Before expanding, validate with real users.

### Phase 1: Closed Beta (Month 1–2)
- Recruit 20–30 engineers via personal network and Zalo groups
- Weekly 30-min Zalo/Google Meet check-ins
- In-app feedback form after each session (NPS + open text)
- Focus questions:
  - Which features do you actually use daily?
  - What's missing for your specific IELTS goal?
  - Would you pay for this? If yes, what price feels fair?
  - Would you recommend to a colleague? Why / why not?

### Phase 2: Expert Review (Month 2–3)
- Recruit 2–3 certified IELTS examiners or prep teachers in Vietnam
- Share the AI scoring output for 10 real essays — ask them to compare AI band vs. their own assessment
- Use findings to calibrate scoring prompts and communicate accuracy in marketing

### Phase 3: Public Early Access (Month 3+)
- Launch a waitlist landing page (simple, mobile-optimised)
- Offer founding-member pricing to the first 100 signups
- Collect structured feedback via Typeform after first 7 days of use

## 7. Pricing Considerations (Vietnam Context)

Vietnamese users are price-sensitive but will pay for clear value. Benchmarks:

| Product | Price (VND/month) |
|---|---|
| IELTS prep course (offline) | 3,000,000 – 10,000,000 (one-time) |
| ELSA Speak | 200,000 – 500,000 |
| Duolingo Max | 350,000 |
| Patreon study group (IELTS KOLs) | 100,000 – 200,000 |

**Suggested model:**
- **Free tier** — Speaking practice (Part 1 only), 3 writing scores/month, vocabulary browser (no AI)
- **Pro — VND 149,000/month** — unlimited scoring, all 4 skills, essay builder, analytics
- **Team (5+ seats) — VND 99,000/seat/month** — for tech companies; billed annually
- **Founding member — VND 99,000/month** locked for life (first 100 users)

## 10. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| AI scoring perceived as inaccurate | Publish expert validation study; show score breakdown with examiner criteria |
| Low organic reach on Facebook | Supplement with small paid boosts (VND 5M/month budget) |
| Users try once and churn | Improve onboarding; add daily streak and email/Zalo reminders |
| Competitors copy the niche angle | Move fast on B2B; build community moat via Zalo group |
| IELTS exam changes format | Monitor British Council/IDP announcements; update prompts within 2 weeks |
